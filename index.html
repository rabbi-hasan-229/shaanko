The Ecosystem

Bridging the Gap

The Problem

The digital goods market in Bangladesh is currently the "Wild West." People are buying digital items through informal Social Media groups, third-party sellers and unverified channels. The result? Constant scams, zero accountability, and honest sellers struggling to build a reputation. Reselling an existing digital item is also difficult due to lack of a trusted and reliable platform.

The Shaako Solution

We built the infrastructure for trust. Shaako acts as the escrow and validator.

  • Mandatory e-KYC Verification for Sellers
  • Secure Payment Escrow System
  • Delivery Confirmation Logic
  • AI-Driven Fraud Pattern Detection

Target Users

Focusing on the digital-native generation of Bangladesh.

Gamers Creators Freelancers Digital Resellers
Monetization
